Our Story

Skomby is a handcrafted health focused kombucha brewed with care in the heart of the Malabar region of Kerala, India.

We offer a refreshing, nourishing drink by combining ancient fermentation techniques with locally sourced ingredients, bringing you the authentic taste and goodness of Kerala in every bottle.
